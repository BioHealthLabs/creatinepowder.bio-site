How to Choose the Right Creatine for Your Goals

Creatine is one of the most researched supplements for improving strength and muscle gain. But choosing the right type of creatine for your body can be tricky. Here's a guide to help:

  • For beginners: Start with creatine monohydrate. It’s the most researched and effective form.
  • For performance: If you need a creatine that absorbs faster with less water retention, try creatine HCl.
  • For muscle gain: Stick with the standard monohydrate, which has been shown to significantly enhance strength and power.
